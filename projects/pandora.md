# Pandora

**Dates:** May 2026 to Present  
**Associated with:** Luna Peak Software  
**URL:** [https://lunapeaksoftware.com/pandora](https://lunapeaksoftware.com/pandora)  
**Repository:** Documentation-first project page

Pandora is ongoing work on an agentic trader model and structured trading workflow system. It explores how NinjaTrader 8 concepts, structured market context, workflows, and human-in-the-loop communication can support strategy development, trade review, decision support, and "do not trade" logic.

## What It Focuses On

- Designing an agentic trader workflow that uses structured market context, NinjaTrader 8 concepts, and human-in-the-loop communication to support trading analysis and decision workflows.
- Building structured telemetry concepts for trend state, market context, risk prompts, setup readiness, and handler communication.
- Exploring workflows where the agent can identify missing setup context, ask for additional information, and support decision discipline.
- Focused on practical trader support: strategy review, journaling, setup validation, and when-not-to-trade logic.

## Boundaries

- Pandora is not presented as a profitable autonomous trading system.
- Pandora is not presented as a live trade execution bot.
- Pandora does not provide investment advice or personalized financial advice.
