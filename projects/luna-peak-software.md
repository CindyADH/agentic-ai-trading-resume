Luna Peak Software is a small software company focused on trading tools, workflow automation, support systems, and AI-assisted product infrastructure.

The live sites were built with agentic AI-assisted development, human review, and hands-on product direction.

## Project Focus

- Building trading-adjacent tools and workflow systems for trader support, internal automation, webhook concepts, and product operations.
- Developing support and delivery infrastructure for small software products.
- Exploring AI-assisted tooling for strategy review, product support, documentation, and user workflows.
- Supporting product operations around onboarding, customer workflows, documentation, release quality, and platform support.

## Why It Matters

Trading tools do not only need code. They need clean product delivery, support systems, documentation, onboarding workflows, release validation, and practical user feedback loops. Luna Peak Software is where these pieces come together.
